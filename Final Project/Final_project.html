Introduction

Golda Meir was an Israeli teacher, kibbutznik, stateswoman, and politician who became the fourth Prime Minister of Israel.

Timeline

  • 1898: Golda Meir is born in Kiev, Ukraine.
  • 1906: Her family immigrates to the United States and settles in Milwaukee, Wisconsin.
  • 1921: She moves to British Mandate Palestine.
  • 1948: Golda plays a significant role in the founding of the State of Israel.
  • 1969: She becomes the first female Prime Minister of Israel.
  • 1978: Golda Meir passes away in Jerusalem.
